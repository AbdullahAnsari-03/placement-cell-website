About Us

Don Bosco

DBIT Training And Placement Cell strongly assimilates the St Don Bosco message to the world i.e. "Help Each Other". We have a student centric approach and we make certain that we connect with as many companies as possible for the placement of our students. Our institute has been blessed with abundance of talent and our Alumni, placed in the industries, have fared very well and are successful in their corporate journeys. ​

DBIT Training and Placement cell is recognized by industries for providing the best trained students. Our placement policy is holistic and well balanced making us a prominent choice for the companies who wish to hold placement drives.

A dedicated and strong T&P team along with the right infrastructure and resources enable us to carry out pooled campus placements efficiently. We are open for placement activities 365 days 24X7 and we assure the Industry, the Students and the Parents that we will not leave any stone upturned to make all our stakeholders happy and proud.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do College Provide any vocational or training course for placements?

Yes, the T&P Cell provides a training course for 5 days before hand with all the necessary subjects and skills. It is compulsory for those students who didn’t cleared the aptitude test which has a benchmark score and those students who clear the aptitude test above the benchmark score have an option for the training course.

When does the placement for the academic year starts?

The placements start from the mid of 6th semester onwards with internship opportunities as well. In case a student misses out an opportunity to get placed the T&P Cell helps the student even after his graduation is completed as we follow the principle of “Help Each Other at Don Bosco”

Does the college provide any guidance/assistance to the students for placements?

Yes, the college T&P Cell works (24*7) 365 days to help the students in the entire placement process and also we have a special trainer onboard who helps the students with interview skills, presentation skills and many more.

Can a Student sit for placements and higher studies both at the same time?

Please read the Student undertaking form for more clearance, available on the website. The guidelines must be strictly followed by the students, if failed to do so the T&P Cell has the entire authority to debar the student from the further placements.
